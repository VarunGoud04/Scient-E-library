Important Questions - Chemistry

Here you will find a list of important questions for Chemistry, which are highly relevant for your B.Tech exams under the JNTUH syllabus.

Physical Chemistry - Important Questions

  • Derive and explain the First Law of Thermodynamics.
  • Explain the concept of chemical equilibrium and Le Chatelier’s Principle.
  • Discuss the concept of chemical kinetics and factors affecting reaction rates.
  • Explain the different types of solutions and their properties.

Inorganic Chemistry - Important Questions

  • Discuss the properties and classification of transition metals.
  • Explain the principles of oxidation-reduction reactions.
  • Describe the structure and bonding in coordination compounds.
  • Explain the concept of crystal field theory and its applications.

Organic Chemistry - Important Questions

  • Discuss the methods of preparation and properties of alkanes and alkenes.
  • Explain the mechanisms of nucleophilic substitution reactions.
  • Describe the concept of aromaticity and its applications in benzene chemistry.
  • Discuss the methods of synthesis of various functional groups like alcohols, ethers, and aldehydes.
